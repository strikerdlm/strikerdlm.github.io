Current Focus

Associate Professor of Aerospace Medicine and lead researcher at the Direction of Aerospace Medicine. My day‑to‑day blends clinical aviation medicine, advanced human‑factors training, and the design of computational tools that turn physiology into actionable signal.

  • Hypobaric chamber & normobaric hypoxia — operational training and physiological research.
  • Spatial disorientation & gyroscopic trainers — sensory illusion mitigation for aircrews.
  • Night vision device integration — visual physiology under low‑light flight conditions.
  • Aviation Medical Examiner (AME) — civil aviation authority certification of pilots and crews.
