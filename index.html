About Me

I am a specialist in Aerospace Medicine from the Universidad Nacional de Colombia, currently serving as a researcher and professor at the Escuela de Posgrados de la Fuerza Aérea Colombiana (FAC). My work focuses on bridging the gap between clinical aerospace medicine and cutting-edge technology, specifically in physiological monitoring and AI-driven performance assessment.

With training from both NASA and ESA, I have participated in analog space missions (THOR simulation) and continue to develop tools for pilots and crews operating in extreme environments, including research initiatives in Antarctica.
