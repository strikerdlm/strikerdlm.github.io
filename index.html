Current Role & Mission

I am an Associate Professor of Aerospace Medicine and specialized researcher at the Direction of Aerospace Medicine. My work bridges the gap between clinical medicine, aviation physiology, and emerging technologies to optimize human performance in high-stakes environments.

As a leading instructor in aerospace physiology, I manage advanced training systems including:

  • Hypobaric Chambers and Normobaric Hypoxia operations.
  • Gyro Integrated Physiology Trainer II (GIPT-II) for Spatial Disorientation training.
  • Night Vision Devices (NVD) and physiological integration.
